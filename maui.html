About Maui

Maori Myths

Maui

Maui is a very clever and daring demi-god. What he did were to help and benefit mankind. He has a superhuman strenght, so he can shapeshift into many animals. His father is Taranga and his mother is Makeatutara. He appears in many myths and legends, like slowing down the sun and catching the North Island of New Zealand.
